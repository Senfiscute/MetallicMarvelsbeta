We make unique metal art. Our pieces are original and can take upwards of 24 hours. Metal art is a beauty unknown to many, each piece is a treasure of its own. We enthusiastically try to satisfy our customers with these hidden gems.

We do not only do predesigned art, we also make custom paintings. Upon request, you can select your own design. This could be for a special occasion like a wedding gift, or for any of your personal desires.
